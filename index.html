 About me

I am a Research Fellow in the Algebra Research Group of the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Sydney, since August 2016.

In the first semesters of 2015 and 2016, I was an instructor at the University of Cologne, while in the second semester of 2015, I was a temporary professor at the University of Bonn.

From May 2009 to April 2015, I was a research fellow at the University of Bonn.

I received my PhD in October 2008 from the University of Cologne. My PhD advisor was Peter Littelmann.

I did my undergraduate studies in Germany, at the University of Wuppertal.
